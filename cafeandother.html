$5 VACATION IN SEOUL!
Last summer, we've been wanting to visit Cafe & Other, a unique summer-y themed cafe in Seongsu.
But we've been putting off till fall, and we finally decided to visit.

Seoul is a big city, a 3/4 of NYC (land size wise), but more population. So, there are people EVERYWHERE.
There also are many wonderful places to eat, tour, and rest.
In Korea, cafes are the most common and popular places to hang and chill.
And Seongsu has just been starting to grow recently to have unique cafes, so I thought we would check one of them out.
Why not chat in a prettier cafe and have more fun?


So, we went to Cafe & Other, which has been famous all summer this year, and still is because...



Because of this pool they have!

When you enter, there is a pool right in the middle of the yard.
A pool in a cafe? How revolutionary!


If you haven't gone on a summer vacation like myself, it might be a good idea to go to this place, take a break from a busy city life for a day, and just chill.





Everyone seemed hesitant to get on the deck that has the pool and sun bed, but when I got there, I walked in with full of confidence and laid on the sun bed. C'mon, isn't this what you came here for?








                     








Look at these drinks. The aesthetic though. Did the cafe owner major in art?
They've got more aesthetic than any artwork I've made in my entire life..



I named this drink a pool water drink because the color matches the water of the pool.
I would've believed if they said they poured it right out of the pool.
Jk. Why would I believe that. I'd probably complain because thousands of people have put their feet in this pool.



After doing a queen-roleplay for a good amount of time with the beautiful set up, we went inside to have a in-depth conversation with Minju, such as “what time is good for you to have fried chicken with me”.

The beautiful yard with a pool isn't it, cafe has first and second floors and a rooftop. And the interior of each floor is very beautiful, so don't forget to check em out!
